About the Initiative

A transatlantic bridge for the future of science.

Crossing Boundaries is more than an academic collaboration; it is a living experiment on how intercultural dialogue catalyzes scientific innovation.

In a globalized world, challenges defined by the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) require solutions that transcend geographical borders. Our methodology unites Chemistry and Microbiology students from Brazil and the UK to develop green technologies.

The COIL Pillars

Collaborative Online International Learning is founded on the intersection of three transformative forces.

Interculturality

Overcoming linguistic boundaries (PT/EN) to create real human connections between Brazil and the UK.

Applied Science

From theory to practice: students developing biopesticides, inoculants, and soil solutions.

SDG Impact

Research driven to solve real-world problems from the UN 2030 Agenda.
