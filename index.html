The COIL Pillars

Our methodology relies on the intersection of three transformative forces.

Interculturality

Overcoming linguistic boundaries (PT/EN) to create real human connections between Brazil and the UK.

Applied Science

From theory to practice: students developing biopesticides and sustainable soil solutions.

SDG Impact

Research driven to solve real-world problems from the UN 2030 Agenda.
