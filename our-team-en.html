Our People

Meet the academic leaders and dedicated researchers from Durham University and UFRJ bridging science and culture.

Project Collaborators

Prof. Andrew Macrae

UFRJ • Microbiology

Current Head of International Relations at the Centre for Health Sciences, UFRJ. He leads research at the Sustainable Biotechnology and Microbial Bioinformatics Lab.

Microbial Ecology Sustainability

Prof. Patrick Steel

Durham • Chemistry

Professor in the Department of Chemistry at Durham University. His work focuses on synthetic organic chemistry and its application to biological problems.

Organic Synthesis Neglected Diseases

Prof. Bruno Lima

IFRN • Language Studies

Full-time Professor at the Federal Institute of Rio Grande do Norte (IFRN). He holds a PhD in Language Studies and specialises in intercultural pedagogy.

COIL Interculturality

Sarah Jenkins

Durham • PhD Candidate

Researching sustainable catalysis methods to reduce industrial waste. She helps coordinate the student exchange programme.

Catalysis Green Chemistry

João Silva

UFRJ • Researcher

Specialises in soil biodiversity and is currently conducting field trials for new bioinoculant formulations in the Atlantic Forest region.

Soil Ecology Bioinoculants

Dr Ana Sousa

Durham • Postdoc

Investigating cross-cultural education barriers in STEM fields, focusing on how language impacts scientific identity formation.

Education STEM

UFRJ • Statistics

Provides statistical support for biological experiments, ensuring data integrity and robust analysis for the project's quantitative outputs.
